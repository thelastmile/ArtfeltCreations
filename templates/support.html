We believe that by teaching our youth to be creative, lessons can transcend into problem solving skills that can have a positive effect on their future.

With funding continuously being cut from art in education across the US, outside support is critical. Incarcerated artist have a chance to give back by donating a percentage of all sales proceeds to fund these programs.
